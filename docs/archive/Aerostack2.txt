🌀 Why Loops Matter
Loops let you:
Track the shape of thinking, not just output


Notice where things stall: before execution, or after feedback?


Capture adaptive growth (Did we change our claim? Our focus? Our model?)


Avoid the illusion of progress when real learning hasn’t occurred



🚨 Trap to Avoid
If you put Aerostack loops into Jira but treat them like tasks:
You’ll re-linearize the system.


You’ll miss the feedback and adaptation that make it agentic.


You'll lose the power of emergent signal as the engine of value.
