## **BLUF (Bottom Line Up Front)**

This is a W2 employment offer for a Growth Associate at enterprise. This role combines volume pipeline generation, customer onboarding, campaign execution, sales ops hygiene, and event visibility into a single high-activity position. Compensation follows enterprise's universal two-component structure (Base + Variable) with commission as the variable mechanism for revenue roles.

This is an early-stage hire in a high-cost market. The base reflects SFO cost of living and the value of joining early. Progression is clear, significant, and tied to measurable outcomes.

## **1. Compensation Structure**

### **Component 1: Base Salary**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Base Salary | $80,000/yr |
| QSEHRA (individual) | $5,520/yr ($460/mo) |
| 401(k) match (3.125%) | $2,500/yr |
| PTO | Per company policy |
| Professional Development | $2,500/yr |
| **Total Fixed W2** | **$90,520/yr** |

*QSEHRA: Qualified Small Employer HRA. enterprise reimburses up to $460/mo individual ($1,000/mo family). Employee must maintain minimum essential coverage. Reimbursement is tax-free for premiums and qualified medical expenses.*

### **Component 2: Commission (Uncapped)**

Commission is calculated on gross revenue (TCV) of closed deals. Uncapped — there is no ceiling on commission earnings.

| Deal Type | Commission Rate |
|-----------|----------------|
| Standard deal | 10% of TCV |
| Strategic deal (1+ criteria met) | 15% of TCV |

**Strategic Criteria** (any one qualifies for the 15% rate):
- **Large Deal:** TCV > $500K
- **Customer Co-Contribution:** Customer pays ≥60% of project cost
- **Strategic Account:** Enterprise MAP customer
- **Proven Expansion:** New contract TCV ≥$50K AND >5× prior total customer realized revenue

*Simplified from the AO/PO dual-track model. This role works all pipeline sources — AWS relationships, strategic channels, referral partners, outbound, campaigns, and events. One rate structure, one commission calculation.*

**Commission Payment:** Paid when revenue recognizes (customer payment clears or AWS funding received). Typically within 30 days of deal close for customer-funded, 45-60 days for AWS-funded.

## **2. MBO Framework (Semi-Annual, SMART)**

MBOs are assessed every 6 months. Target payout: 20% of base salary ($16,000/yr, $8,000 per half).

Attainment levels:
- 0-70%: No payout
- 70-90%: Pro-rated payout
- 90-110%: Full payout
- 110%+: Capped at 1.1× ($8,800 per half max)

MBOs are **SMART** (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound) and **iterable** — adjusted at each semi-annual boundary based on business pace, demand, and role evolution.

## **3. Role Responsibilities**

This is not a pure closer role. This is a **volume + onboarding + visibility** role that touches multiple parts of the revenue engine:

**Pipeline Generation & Deal Execution (40%)**
- Source, develop, and close AWS consulting opportunities across all channels
- Work AWS partner relationships, strategic channels, referral partners, outbound, and events
- Run consistent weekly outreach and qualified first calls
- Navigate AWS programs and co-sell motions with AWS AEs and SAs

**Customer Onboarding (25%)**
- Own the customer onboarding experience from signed deal through kickoff
- Coordinate with delivery teams on scoping, pricing, and customer handoff
- Ensure smooth transition using the Project Handoff tool in Aerostack
- Maintain clean CRM attribution and deal documentation

**Campaigns & Sales Ops (20%)**
- Execute outbound campaigns aligned to GTM strategy
- Maintain pipeline hygiene — stage accuracy, next steps, close dates
- Support sales ops automation as processes move into Aerostack
- Manage MDF applications and AWS marketing coordination

**Visibility & Events (15%)**
- Represent enterprise at meetups, AWS events, community events, and conferences
- Support event logistics and follow-up
- Contribute to thought leadership content (LinkedIn, blog, case studies)

## **5. Progression Path**

### **L4 → L5 (6-9 months, significant)**

This is an early-stage hire in a high-cost market. Progression is designed to be meaningful and fast for high performers.

**Requirements:**
- 2+ consecutive quarters at or above activity targets
- Close 8+ deals over 6 months
- Average deal size ≥ $25K
- At least 2 strategic deals (meeting 1+ criterion)
- Showcasenstrated campaign execution and onboarding ownership
- MBO attainment ≥90% for 2 consecutive periods

**Compensation Change:**

| Component | L4 (Current) | L5 (Promotion) |
|-----------|-------------|----------------|
| Base Salary | $80,000 | $100,000 (+25%) |
| MBO Target | $16,000 (20%) | $20,000 (20%) |
| Commission | 10% / 15% strategic | 10% / 15% strategic |
| QSEHRA | $5,520 | $5,520 |
| Prof Dev | $2,500 | $3,000 |
| **Total Fixed W2** | **$90,520** | **$111,520** |

*$20K base increase reflects SFO cost of living, early-stage contribution value, and showcasenstrated impact. This is not a token raise — it's a structural recognition of the value created.*

### **L5 → L6 (12-18 months)**

| Component | L5 | L6 |
|-----------|----|----|
| Base Salary | $100,000 | $115,000 |
| MBO Target | $20,000 | $23,000 |
| Commission | 10% / 15% | Tiered: 8% owned + 3% influenced |
| PI Eligibility | Vesting begins | Active vesting |
| **Total Fixed W2** | **$111,520** | **$128,520** |

*At L6, W2 is strongly preferred per framework. PI vesting activates. Commission evolves to tiered model recognizing ecosystem leverage — deals you close directly AND deals closed by partners/channels you built.*
